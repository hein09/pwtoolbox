Two sets of widgets are keen to serve your every needs:

Basic functions

Molecule/Cell settings

Edit basic properties of a Molecule/Cell/Trajectory.

  • Coordinates are accessible in multiple formats
  • Cell modifications can keep either the real or the crystal-coordinates intact
  • Multiple ways of expressing the k-point grid

Parameter sets

Parameter sets for PWScf or CPMD can be saved/passed through/edited.

Additionally, preference for a LAMMPS data file can be managed

Periodic table

Manage global or file-specific settings for your atom-types.

  • Graphical representation
  • Physical properties
  • PWScf/CPMD properties

Settings

Change everything that's not specific to a file/species.

Additional tools

Picker

View/analyze your selection of atoms.

Cell Modifications

  • Wrap/crop
  • Multiply
  • Reshape
  • Align to axis

Script

Modify the structure programmatically:

  • Rotation
  • Shift
  • Mirroring
  • Select
  • Align to surface

Plane

Display crystal-planes according to their miller-indices or visualize a slice of volume data.

Volume

Display isosurfaces of some volume data.
