The vipster.Molecule class is the main data container of Vipster.

It is the interface to one or multiple instances of vipster._step, depending on whether a single configuration or a trajectory needs to be represented. The access to the data can happen either transparently (via the Molecule's members and the active Molecule.curStep) or by accessing the elements of Molecule.steps directly.
